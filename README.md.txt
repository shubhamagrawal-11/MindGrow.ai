AI-powered gamified learning platform with mood tracking, growth analytics, role-based dashboards, and an intelligent study companion. MindGrow.AI AI-Powered Gamified Learning & Growth Platform 🚀 Overview

MindGrow.AI is a gamified, AI-assisted learning platform designed to improve student focus, consistency, and performance through:

📚 Interactive quizzes

📊 Growth analytics

🎯 Focus tools

😊 Mood tracking

🏆 XP, streaks, and badges

🤖 Astra AI Assistant (personal learning companion)

The system supports role-based dashboards for Students and Teachers.

🎯 Problem Statement

Students struggle with:

Low engagement

Lack of motivation

No personalized feedback

Poor focus tracking

Limited growth visibility

Teachers struggle with:

Monitoring student consistency

Identifying performance gaps

Tracking learning trends

💡 Our Solution

MindGrow.AI combines:

Gamification psychology (XP, streaks, rewards)

Behavioral tracking (mood + consistency)

Analytics dashboard

AI assistant (Astra) for personalized feedback

Clean, modular front-end architecture
